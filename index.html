About Dr. Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). Widely known as the "Missile Man of India" for his pivotal role in India's missile development, Dr. Kalam inspired millions with his humility, vision, and relentless dedication to the nation.

He was instrumental in advancing India's space and defense programs, including leading the development of ballistic missile and space launch technologies. Dr. Kalam's efforts in the 1998 nuclear tests made India a nuclear power.

Beyond science, he authored inspirational books like *Wings of Fire* and *Ignited Minds*. His speeches and writings continue to motivate the youth of India to dream big and work hard for a better future.
